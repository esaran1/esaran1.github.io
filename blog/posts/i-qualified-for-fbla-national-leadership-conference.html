I Qualified for the FBLA National Leadership Conference

March 2026 · 3 min read

I just found out I qualified for the FBLA National Leadership Conference in the Introduction to Programming event, and I'm really excited about it.

NLC is being held in San Antonio this year. Introduction to Programming is a presentation event, so qualifying meant putting together something compelling enough to stand out at the state level and make the case in front of judges. That's a different kind of pressure than a written test or coding challenge, and it's one I'm glad I leaned into.

It's a cool milestone. San Antonio, see you soon.
